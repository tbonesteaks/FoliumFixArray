Denver Police Data Visualized Using Python, Folium, & HeatMap

This data is politically charged, so I will not be adding an explanation with these maps and visualizations. This data is capable of speaking for itself, and viewers are encouraged to draw their own conclusions. This is not a Rorschach test. This is to show Folium's capability of illustrating, via Heat Map, half a million coordinates on a map under 15mb. The automation and backend programming are shared under the MIT license. Top links display whole dataset maps (500,000+ Plot Points). Links below are separated by year, include access to the raw dataset source, and a tutorial.
